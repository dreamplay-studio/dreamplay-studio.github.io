Harshvardhan

DIRECTOR | VISIONARY

Harshvardhan of Jaycees Public School is a young filmmaker and game developer passionate about films, animation, AI, and storytelling. His vision at DreamPlay Studio is to bridge the gap between emotion and technology.Harshvardhan is the Founder of DreamPlay Studio, established on 1 July 2026. He is a young filmmaker, storyteller, game developer, and creative creator with a strong passion for building original cinematic experiences. His vision is to create meaningful films, animations, and interactive games that inspire audiences while blending technology with powerful storytelling. From an early age, Harshvardhan developed a deep interest in filmmaking, animation, artificial intelligence, robotics, and game development. He enjoys transforming imaginative ideas into engaging visual experiences, whether through short films, cinematic trailers, or immersive games. He believes that every story has the power to educate, entertain, and create a lasting emotional impact. As the founder of DreamPlay Studio, Harshvardhan focuses on writing original stories, designing concepts, directing creative projects, and exploring innovative production techniques. He continuously learns new technologies and creative skills to improve his work and deliver high-quality content. His long-term goal is to build DreamPlay Studio into a globally recognized production house known for original storytelling, cinematic excellence, and innovation. Through dedication, creativity, and a passion for learning, Harshvardhan hopes to create projects that connect with audiences around the world and leave a lasting impression through films, animation, and interactive entertainment.

Arnav Tyagi

EDITING EXPERT

Arnav Tyagi of Jaycees Public School is the Editing Expert and Creative Support of DreamPlay Studio. Known for his surgical precision and rhythmic style, Arnav ensures every project produced carries a cinematic soul.Arnav has a strong interest in video editing, visual effects, motion graphics, and content presentation. He enjoys enhancing films, trailers, and promotional content by combining creativity with technical precision. His work focuses on creating smooth transitions, impactful pacing, cinematic color grading, and visually appealing edits that strengthen the emotional impact of every story. As Co-Founder, Arnav collaborates closely with the creative team during production and post-production, helping maintain high-quality standards across all DreamPlay Studio projects. He believes that effective editing is one of the most important elements of storytelling, bringing scenes together in a way that captivates audiences. With a passion for continuous learning and innovation, Arnav aims to contribute to DreamPlay Studio's vision of producing original films, animations, games, and digital content that inspire, entertain, and leave a lasting impression on audiences worldwide.
